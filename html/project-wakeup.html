Wake Up – Three Thing Game (May 2018)

The Three Thing Game is a 24-hour Game Jam, hosted by the Computer Science department at the University of Hull. It takes place twice a year. Teams are given three random words which are to be somehow implemented into their game.

My team consisted of 4 people: Anthony Beacock (myself), Jakob Scrivener, Dean Barnard and Reece Giles (who stayed for the Friday night), and our words were “Hopping”, “Sheeple” and “Building”.
